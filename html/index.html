Welcome to the World of Super Mario!

Super Mario is a classic video game series created by Nintendo in the 1980s. The series follows the adventures of Mario, a small Italian plumber, and his brother Luigi as they travel through the Mushroom Kingdom to rescue Princess Peach from the evil Bowser. The series has sold over 500 million copies worldwide and is one of the most successful and recognizable video game franchises of all time.
